The Best Movies Of All Time

My Top Three Movies

The Man Who Knew Infinity

A biography of one of the greatest mathematicians of all times Srinivasa Ramanujan. An inspiring movie that kindles the feelings of sorrow , happiness, determination and the zeal to achieve the greatest heights with unwavering faith in oneself. A must watch movie for those who are inclined towards learning from not only success but also from the adverse situations faced in the lives of such great personalities

Tare Zameen Par

A motivation for not just students but also for parents. A new and refreshing perspective about education and the necessity of bringing out the innate talents in children and supporting them in their endeavors

Dangal

Women empowerment. A splendid example of how women have excelled even in the field of sports
